Rafael Cortes, also known as Rafa, is a 16 year old photographer and filmmaker residing in Boulder, Colorado. His work leans into scale, geometry, and atmosphere to make everyday spaces feel cinematic.

The site takes cues from editorial artist pages: oversized typography, strong framing, and carefully paced imagery. The goal is to let each photo feel intentional while keeping the overall identity sharp and memorable.

About

Young eye. Controlled frame.

Rafa is developing a visual language rooted in contrast, structure, and mood. Whether he is shooting architecture, urban detail, or motion-led film work, the focus stays the same: build a frame with presence.

Based in Boulder, he brings a clean graphic instinct to image-making and balances it with a documentary sense of place. The result is work that feels both modern and personal.
